Findings

Two clear results, two

Sample queries

What the eval set

Three representative items — one pass, one needs-review, one fail.

B13.4 recall ✓PASS judge confidence 0.92
What is the MF cost for an infantry squad to enter a woods hex from open ground?
EXPECTED
2 MF, regardless of hexside crossed (B13.4). Road rate does not apply.
MODEL RESPONSE
Entering a Woods hex costs 2 MF for non-Cavalry infantry (B13.4). If a road runs through the woods, the road rate B3.4 does not apply because Woods overrides road effects.
A12.1 recall ⚠NEEDS REVIEW judge confidence 0.61
When a Concealment counter is removed, does the unit also lose Hidden Initiative status?
EXPECTED
No. Concealment loss does not alter Hidden Initiative; the two are tracked independently (A12.1).
MODEL RESPONSE
Yes — when a Concealment counter is lost, the unit also loses Hidden Initiative because they share the same trigger. (A4.34)
C8.31 calc ✕FAIL judge confidence 0.84
A 4-6-7 fires at an unconcealed 4-4-7 in a Stone Building, range 4, no other DRM. What FP column and DRM?
EXPECTED
4 FP column, +3 TEM (Stone Building), +1 long-range = +4 net DRM; 4 (1) column on IFT.
MODEL RESPONSE
4 FP column, +3 TEM, no range DRM. Net +3 DRM on 4 (1) column.
