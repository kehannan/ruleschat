01
Findings

Two clear results, two
open questions.

  1. i
    The latest models answer recall reliably.
    On this 213-question eval, the top two models clear 95% on pure rule lookups. Next step: a harder eval — currently being built — to find where they actually start to break.
  2. ii
    Calculations are where the cheap models fail.
    Calc accuracy spreads 38–86% across the lineup. Next step: failure-mode analysis on calc errors to see whether the wall is retrieval, reasoning, or arithmetic — then target the weakest link.

A4.5 recall ✓PASS judge confidence 0.95
How many additional MF does Double Time provide to Infantry?
EXPECTED
Double Time increases the MF allotment of Infantry by two. If announced after a unit has expended MF, it increases the unit's MF by only one while incurring the same penalties.
MODEL RESPONSE
Double Time provides Infantry with +2 MF, increasing the normal allotment by two; if declared after some MF have already been expended, it provides only +1 MF.
A12.1 recall ⚠NEEDS REVIEW judge confidence 0.61
When a Concealment counter is removed, does the unit also lose Hidden Initiative status?
EXPECTED
No. Concealment loss does not alter Hidden Initiative; the two are tracked independently (A12.1).
MODEL RESPONSE
Yes — when a Concealment counter is lost, the unit also loses Hidden Initiative because they share the same trigger. (A4.34)
C8.31 calc ✕FAIL judge confidence 0.84
A 4-6-7 fires at an unconcealed 4-4-7 in a Stone Building, range 4, no other DRM. What FP column and DRM?
EXPECTED
4 FP column, +3 TEM (Stone Building), +1 long-range = +4 net DRM; 4 (1) column on IFT.
MODEL RESPONSE
4 FP column, +3 TEM, no range DRM. Net +3 DRM on 4 (1) column.
