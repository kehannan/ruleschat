gpt-5-mini is the clear winner on straightforward questions. For questions that are well-scoped and not especially difficult, recall accuracy hits 99% — the model reliably retrieves and applies rule text even when the answer spans multiple rules. Calc accuracy jumped to 86%, more than doubling gpt-4.1-mini's 50%. These numbers reflect questions that are clear and answerable; harder scenarios involving edge-case interactions, ambiguous rule conflicts, or deep chained reasoning across sections are not yet in this eval set and would likely show lower accuracy. The trade-off is speed: injecting up to 20 retrieved chunks (~20k tokens) into every query adds significant latency.

All results confirmed by human review. The AI Judge flags borderline answers for manual verification before they are counted.
