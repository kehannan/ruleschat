Q.
How many additional MF does Double Time provide to Infantry?
A.
Double Time increases an Infantry unit's MF allotment by +2 MF (A4.5). If declared after the unit has already expended MF, the bonus drops to +1 while the same penalties still apply.
The domain

Advanced Squad Leader has
a 700-page rulebook.

Advanced Squad Leader is a tactical WWII wargame that was historically played on hex maps with cardboard counters and dice. Most play has moved online through VASSAL and VASL — but those platforms only move counters and roll dice. Every rule interpretation, modifier, and edge case still falls on the players.

The agent loop

The model shows its work.

Every question goes to an agent with a toolbelt: rulebook search, exact section lookup (with official Q&A and errata), and deterministic combat calculators. The model decides per question which tools to use, and keeps looking things up until it has the rule text in hand.

Answers cite their sources. Clicking a citation like A8.2 opens the rulebook PDF to that page, and a cite-check pass can re-read every cited section against the draft before it ships.

The payoff is measurable: on the evals, giving the model tools is worth about 30 points on calculation questions.
