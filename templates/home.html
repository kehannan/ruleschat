Q.
How many additional MF does Double Time provide to Infantry?
A.
Double Time increases an Infantry unit's MF allotment by +2 MF (A4.5). If declared after the unit has already expended MF, the bonus drops to +1 while the same penalties still apply.
The domain

Advanced Squad Leader has
a 700-page rulebook.

Advanced Squad Leader is a tactical WWII wargame that was historically played on hex maps with cardboard counters and dice. Most play has moved online through VASSAL and VASL — but those platforms only move counters and roll dice. Every rule interpretation, modifier, and edge case still falls on the players.

The pipeline

How it works.

i
Stage 01 · Retrieval

RAG over the rulebook

Each question is routed through OpenRouter to one of several models, which pulls relevant chunks from a vector store of the rulebook.

OpenRouterfile_searchmulti-model
ii
Stage 02 · Grounding

Cited answers

Every claim links to a rule section, and clicking a citation like A4.34 opens the rulebook PDF to that page in-browser.

section_pages.jsonpdf.js
iii
Stage 03 · Agentic

Tools, then verification

The model works in a loop: deterministic IFT / CC calculators do the arithmetic, a lookup tool fetches the exact text (plus official Q&A) of any rule before it's cited, and a cite-check pass re-reads every cited section and corrects the draft.

function callingget_sectioncite-check
iv
Stage 04 · Evaluation

LLM-as-a-judge

A zero-shot AI judge scores each response Pass / Fail / Needs Review against expected answers, with manual review before results are published.

v
Stage 05 · Improvement

Curate & retrain

Strong Q&A pairs feed eval datasets and fine-tuning corpora; weak ones become test cases.
